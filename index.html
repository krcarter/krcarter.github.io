Hello, my name is Kamal Carter a lifelong resident of Philadelphia, and current Masters student at Carnegie Mellon University. I have a bachelor of science in mechanical engineering with a minor in robotics. My talents are with robotic, and I focus on the mechanical design, fabrication, and CAD. As a high school student, I was a member of my FRC robotics team for four strong years, leading fabrication and design for my team. Currently I am an graduate research assistant in the Carnegie Mellon University Robomechanics lab. I have a endearing passion to promote STEM in my community whether that be in Philadelphia, my school, or the world.
